# MASH

The classic fortune-telling game, right in your browser. 90s notebook paper vibes included.

**Play it:** [ashleywolf.github.io/mash-game](https://ashleywolf.github.io/mash-game)

## How to play

1. Fill in your options (or keep the fun defaults)
2. Draw a spiral to get your magic number
3. Watch destiny cross out your future, one option at a time
4. Read your fortune

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, just vibes.
